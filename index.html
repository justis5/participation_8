Historical context

Two major advances in the past contributed to the modern war machine. Advances in technology during the Industrial Revolution made weapons and machines exponentially deadlier and effective. After The French Revolution, the concept of a people in arms would allow nations to mobilize and field armies exceeding those of the past. Welcome to modern warfare.
